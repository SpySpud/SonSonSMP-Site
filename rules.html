🧱 Server Rules

⚔️ Survival SMP Server
A competitive survival experience where PvP, raiding and griefing are allowed.

🤝 Respect Everyone

Treat all players and staff with respect. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, threats and excessive toxicity are not allowed.

🚫 No Cheating

Any form of cheating or gaining an unfair advantage is prohibited.

  • Hacked clients
  • X-Ray
  • Auto-clickers
  • Macros
  • Combat advantages
  • Exploits
  • Unauthorized modifications

🐛 No Exploiting

Do not abuse bugs, glitches, duplication exploits, or unintended game mechanics.

⚔️ Raiding, Griefing & Stealing

Raiding, griefing and stealing are allowed.

  • Do not use bugs or exploits to raid
  • Do not intentionally crash the server
  • Do not create lag machines
  • Do not abuse unintended mechanics

💬 Keep Chat Appropriate

  • No spam
  • No excessive profanity
  • No harassment
  • No threats
  • No inappropriate content

🏗️ Appropriate Builds Only

Offensive, hateful, NSFW, or inappropriate builds are not allowed.

👥 No Alt Abuse

Alternate accounts may not be used to evade punishments, bypass bans, or gain unfair advantages.

🛡️ Listen To Staff

Staff decisions must be respected.

  • Do not threaten staff
  • Do not harass staff
  • Do not target staff members
  • Do not repeatedly disrespect staff

⚖️ Punishment System

⛏️ X-Ray Punishments

  • 1st Offence: 1 Hour Ban + Ore Wipe
  • 2nd Offence: 6 Hour Ban + Inventory Wipe
  • 3rd Offence: 2 Day Ban
  • 4th Offence: Permanent Ban

🛡️ Hacking Punishments

  • 1st Offence: 24 Hour Ban
  • 2nd Offence: 12 Day Ban
  • 3rd Offence: Permanent Ban

🚨 Server Damage / Exploit Abuse

Using exploits, dupes, lag machines, or methods intended to harm the server may result in punishment. Severe cases may result in a permanent ban.

📌 Final Notes

  • Staff may adjust punishments when necessary.
  • Severe violations may skip punishment stages.
  • Ban evasion may result in additional penalties.
  • By playing, you agree to follow these rules.
